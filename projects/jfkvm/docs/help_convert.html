jfKVM

Convert:
========

You can convert .vmx systems to jfKVM.

Using filezilla you can upload a vmware system to one of your pools.

From the Storage pool page click on a pool and click on Browse.
Find the .vmx and click on Register.

The conversion process will find existing disks and create a basic Linux system.
Customize as needed and save.

In the future it will most likely be best to create new NFS storage pools and
then use VMWare vCenter to perform a data storage migration from VMFS pools to NFS pools.
