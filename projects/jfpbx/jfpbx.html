jfPBX


A lightweight VoIP/SIP PBX.

To configure the PBX open your browser to http://localhost:8080/jfpbx

Initial username/password is admin/admin to login.

After you login click on warning messages to clear them.

Make sure to enter in your local IP address in the settings page.

Now configure your extensions and trunks.
